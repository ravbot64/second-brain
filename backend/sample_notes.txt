PRODUCT DESIGN NOTES - 2026-03-11

Concept: "Second Brain" App
- It should integrate with systems like Notion and Apple Notes.
- Key differentiator: semantic search and agentic AI behaviors instead of simple RAG.
- Architecture uses FastAPI, Next.js, and local Embeddings using sentence-transformers (all-MiniLM-L6-v2) for ultra-fast performance.
- Qdrant is the chosen Vector Database because of its Rust-based speed and easy local SQLite persistence setup.

Meeting Notes with Engineering Team (March 10):
- Discussed handling chunking sizes. We settled on 500-word chunks with 50-word overlap initially.
- Explored hybrid search, eventually we might add BM25.
- Connectors architecture: Built a BaseConnector interface so adding new sources like Obsidian or Google Drive later is trivial.

User Feedback from Prototype:
"I love the glassmorphism dark theme UI! It feels very premium and instantly sets apart from basic boilerplate Chat apps."
